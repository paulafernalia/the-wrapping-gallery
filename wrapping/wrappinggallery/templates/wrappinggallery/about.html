WELCOME

My name is Paula and I started wrapping my daughter in November 2022, when she was only 5 months old. I learnt to wrap thanks to the wonderful people in this community who dedicated a lot of time and effort to creating great educational videos and resources and to answering questions in babywearing groups.

I want to give back to the community by creating a space where us wrappers can feel inspired to try new carries, find the perfect carry for any occasion and escape the temptation to buy new wraps.

The Wrapping Gallery will never be affiliated with any brand and access to all content in this site is and always will be free. If you choose to support the project, whether to help cover the costs of the server, data storage and the domain, or to acknowledge the time I dedicate to creating the content, any contributions will be welcome, but completely voluntary.

This is an evolving project. If you have any feedback, suggestion, complaint, or spot any errors email me at thewrappinggallery@gmail.com. If you are tech-savvy and would like to contribute to the code, you can find my public repo here.

May The Wrapping Gallery inspire and support you in your wrapping journey just as much as I am enjoying creating it.
