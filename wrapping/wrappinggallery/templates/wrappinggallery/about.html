HELLO, I'M PAULA

I am a first time mum and a wrapper. I draw and code when my daughter is sleeping. Since December 2022, woven wraps have been an integral part of my relationship with her.

I have embarked on a mission to create a hand-drawn tutorial for every documented woven wrap carry and a place where you feel inspired to try new things and where you can find the perfect carry for any occasion.

May The Wrapping Gallery inspire and support you in your wrapping journey just as much as I am enjoying creating it.

This is a work in progress. If you have any ideas or spot any errors or would just like to chat about this, email me at thewrappinggallery@gmail.com.

If you are a web developer and would like to help me out, I have made this project open source; you can find everything in my GitHub repo the-wrapping-gallery.
